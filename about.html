About Us

Rooted in community, focused on care

Green Acres Healthcare Services LLC was founded to bring dependable, compassionate home care to families across Georgia — grounded in careful compliance and genuine attention to the people we serve.

Our Story

Green Acres Healthcare Services LLC is a Georgia-based private duty home care agency, regulated by the Healthcare Facility Regulation Division (HFRD). We built this agency around a simple belief: people recover, live, and thrive best in the comfort of their own homes, surrounded by consistent, trustworthy care.

Every policy, training program, and client agreement we use is built to meet Georgia's regulatory standards — not as a formality, but because families deserve an agency that takes accountability seriously.

Leadership

Corneille — Owner & Designated Administrator

Corneille holds an MBA in Finance and Risk Management from Georgia State University and brings a background in enterprise risk management from the financial sector to the operation of Green Acres Healthcare Services. As Designated Administrator, Corneille oversees regulatory compliance, quality of care, and day-to-day operations of the agency.
