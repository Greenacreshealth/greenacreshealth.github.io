Certified Nursing Assistant (CNA)

Green Acres Healthcare Services LLC is seeking a compassionate, reliable Certified Nursing Assistant (CNA) to provide direct, hands-on care to clients in their homes. The CNA works under the supervision of a Registered Nurse to support clients' daily living needs, promote comfort and dignity, and help clients remain safely in their home environment.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist clients with activities of daily living (ADLs), including bathing, grooming, dressing, and toileting
  • Assist with mobility, transfers, and positioning
  • Take and record vital signs (temperature, pulse, respiration, blood pressure)
  • Assist with feeding and monitor nutritional/fluid intake as directed
  • Perform light housekeeping related to client care (changing linens, tidying care areas)
  • Observe and report changes in client condition to the supervising nurse
  • Maintain accurate, timely documentation of care provided
  • Follow the individualized care plan and all agency policies and procedures
  • Maintain client confidentiality in accordance with HIPAA

Qualifications

  • Current, unencumbered Georgia CNA certification (on the Georgia Nurse Aide Registry)
  • High school diploma or GED preferred
  • CPR certification (or willingness to obtain)
  • Reliable transportation and valid driver's license
  • Ability to pass a background check and drug screening per HFRD requirements
  • Prior home care or pediatric care experience a plus
  • Strong communication skills and a caring, patient demeanor

Physical Requirements

  • Ability to lift, transfer, and assist clients as needed; stand, bend, and move throughout a shift.

Home Health Aide

Green Acres Healthcare Services LLC is looking for a dependable and caring Home Health Aide (HHA) to support clients with personal care and daily living activities in their homes. The HHA plays a vital role in helping clients maintain independence, comfort, and quality of life, working under the direction of the agency's supervising nurse.

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist clients with personal hygiene, bathing, grooming, and dressing
  • Help with mobility, transfers, and safe ambulation
  • Prepare meals and assist with feeding as needed
  • Perform light housekeeping and laundry related to the client's care
  • Provide companionship and emotional support
  • Monitor and report any changes in the client's condition or home environment
  • Accurately document services provided during each visit
  • Accurately document services provided during each visit
  • Maintain confidentiality of client information in accordance with HIPAA

Qualifications

  • Completion of a state-approved Home Health Aide training program (or CNA certification)
  • High school diploma or GED preferred
  • CPR certification (or willingness to obtain)
  • Reliable transportation and valid driver's license
  • Ability to pass a background check and drug screening per HFRD requirements
  • Compassionate, patient, and dependable with strong communication skills
  • Prior home care experience preferred

Physical Requirements

  • Ability to lift, transfer, and assist clients as needed; stand, bend, and move throughout a shift.
