Services

Care built around each person's needs

From adult companionship to specialized pediatric support, our services are tailored, licensed, and delivered where people are most comfortable — at home.

Adult Services

Private Duty Home Care

Our private duty caregivers support adults with daily living activities, companionship, and non-medical or skilled care needs, allowing clients to remain safely and comfortably at home.

  • Personal care assistance (bathing, dressing, mobility)
  • Companionship and light housekeeping
  • Medication reminders and health monitoring support
  • Flexible scheduling — hourly to live-in care
Eligibility

How services are authorized

Services are generally authorized through a physician's order and, for GAPP services, coordination with your Medicaid Care Management Organization (CMO) or case manager. Our team will help confirm eligibility and walk you through the authorization process during your initial consultation.
